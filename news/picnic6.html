Important Summer Picnic Update

It has come to my attention that our flier and some of our previous emails were not very clear as to the nature of our upcoming Summer Picnic. This is not a free event, as some of our previous picnic's have been. This year we opted for a catered event as this is a very necessary fundraiser to help support our candidates for the 2016 General Election.

Advanced registration is required as we need to know how much food to have ready for the adults and kids that will attend, as well as how many will want to participate in trap shooting and the other planned activities.

Registration with payment online at the link below is the quickest option, but at the very least contact us via email at: comcentral@wash-gop.org and let us know your dinner and trap shooting preferences (use the registration form at the link below) and you can then pay at the event. But please contact us to let us know your intentions, mailing the form at this point would reach us too late.

It's sure to be a wonderful event, certainly one you won't want to miss!
