Washtenaw County Republican Party


Fellow Republicans,

This is not by way of endorsement but as a Republican candidate for President I thought I'd share the following information regarding his local fundraiser and free-to-attend rally.

Presidential candidate and U-M graduate Ben Carson returning to Ann Arbor

Retired pediatric neurosurgeon and
Republican presidential candidate Ben Carson will return to his old stomping grounds next week when he visits Ann Arbor and Ypsilanti.

Carson will be in the region December 9th as he makes a one-day campaign visit to Washtenaw County.

The University of Michigan Medical School graduate will host a fundraising luncheon at the Ann Arbor Marriott Ypsilanti at Eagle Crest from 12:30 p.m. until 1:30 p.m.

Tickets to the luncheon are still available via an eventbrite website (click here)  and require a $500 per person donation to the Carson campaign. Those wishing to attend a VIP reception at noon can contribute $1,000, and "hosts" are asked to donate $2,700 per person, the maximum allowable campaign contribution.

There will also be a free public meeting/rally with Dr. Ben Carson December 9th at Eastern Michigan University Convocation from 2:30 to 4:00 p.m. Tickets are free and are available at www.bencarson.com under the events tab.
