4th of July Parades
Join the Washtenaw County Republican Party and some of our great Republican candidates this 4th of July for a parade!  We have candidates walking in the Ann Arbor & Whitmore Lake Parades and would love for you to join us!

Register today at the link below so we'll know you'll be attending and so we can update you with important line-up information.

Please RSVP at the following link:  http://www.wash-gop.com/parades

We'll have contingents as well as vehicles in both parades that can accommodate those that might not be able to walk the whole route.  Phil Chinn will be driving his Mustang Convertible with the top down, flags flying and side signs in Whitmore Lake and we will have Matt Cauley's Antique Fire engine and trailer leading our contingency in Ann Arbor. We would love for you to join us, celebrate our Independence and hand out candy to the children along the route. Everyone's welcome, bring the whole family!

The parades generally start line-up around 9:00 AM with the actual parade starting at 10:00 AM and we'll most likely be done by noon.
