Join Us for Some Summer Fun!

Hi there!

We thought we would take a minute to let you know about some of the activities we’re planning for our summer picnic on August 21st, 2016, just a week from Sunday.  We’re working hard to make sure that our event has a bit of “something for everybody”; here is a list of what we’re planning:

First, as you’ll see from the sign-up sheet, a Trap shooting range will be open for your use.  If you want to sharpen up your own marksman skills, of if you’d like to introduce family members to shooting and gun safety issues, this will provide that opportunity.  And now …

For the kids (and the young at heart):

Kite construction: We have a kite enthusiast who is going to conduct a Kite Building Session where participants will construct a “tail-less” diamond kite; he assures us that these kites are excellent fliers, and if we have any kind of wind at all we’ll hope to be able to demonstrate that.

Bean bags: We’ll have a “maize and blue” bean bag setup for a little friendly competition

Water balloon toss: It’s likely to be a hot August Sunday afternoon, so how about a little water balloon tossing to demonstrate your throwing and catching skills? [They say Republicans are “all wet” anyway, so why not practice it?]

“Thread the Needle”: This is a relay race (of sorts) involving hula hoops.  Need we say more?

“Slammer Jammer”:  What would a summer day be without a little Frisbee tossing?  In this case, teams of two try to precisely position the landing spot of a thrown Frisbee.

Coloring books: For the real little ones, we’re going to have coloring books and crayons.

Swings and Slide: And an outing wouldn't be complete without swooshing down a slide or some enjoyable time on a swing.

For adults (and kids who want to be adults)

Horseshoe Toss: We’ll have regulation horse shoe pits set up for those who want to hone their skills

Bocci Ball: Giuseppe Garibaldi [the father of modern Italy] introduced this sport in the late 1800s and it is now played all around the world.  Form a couple of teams and see who can get closest to "the jack”!

Hillary Scandal Bingo: It takes 24 different items to fill up a bingo card and guess what: we didn’t have any trouble at all finding 24 Clinton Scandals to play this game.  Come on out and have some fun all the while re-learning just how appalling the Democrats’ candidate is.

Selife photos Yup, we’re going to have a Selfie Photo Station set up so you can fool around with this popular new way of generating content for your phone or social media.

The Vinyl Groove: All of this, and a live band, too!  And guess what?  We won’t stop you if you want to do a little dancing!

This will be a great event, certainly one you won't want to miss!
