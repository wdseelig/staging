4th of July Parades
Join the Washtenaw County Republican Party and some of our great Republican candidates this 4th of July for a parade!  We have candidates walking in the Ann Arbor & Whitmore Lake Parades and would love for you to join us!

We'll have contingents as well as vehicles in both parades that can accommodate those that might not be able to walk the whole route.  Phil Chinn will be driving his Mustang Convertible with the top down, flags flying and side signs in Whitmore Lake and we will have Matt Cauley's Antique Fire engine and trailer leading our contingency in Ann Arbor. We would love for you to join us, celebrate our Independence and hand out candy to the children along the route. Everyone's welcome, bring the whole family!

The parades generally start line-up around 9:00 AM with the actual parade starting at 10:00 AM and we'll most likely be done by noon.

Click on the link below to RSVP for a specific parade and we'll update you this weekend with further line-up information.

August 2nd Primary Election
  1. All cities and townships across Michigan will hold primary elections on Tuesday, August 2, 2016.

  2. Election Inspectors are in great need and training sessions are coming up soon. Email us directly if you'd like to serve at comcentral@wash-gop.org

  3. The deadline to register for the August Primary is Tuesday, July 5th by 5:00 PM - if you know of any new young people that have turned 18 recently, please encourage them to register to vote!

  4. Go to http://www.Michigan.gov/vote to view your sample ballot, request an absentee ballot or just to see if you're registered to vote.

August Conventions
Washtenaw County Convention will be held on: Thursday, August 11 at 7:00 pm (location yet to be determined)

 The purpose of the County Conventions will be the selection of delegates and alternates to the August 26-27, 2016 State Convention at the DeVos Place in Grand Rapids, Michigan.

The State Convention of the delegated representatives of the Republican Party of Michigan will be held on the following purposes:
  1. The election of sixteen (16) Presidential Electors
  2. The Nomination of:
    1. Two (2) Justices to the Michigan Supreme Court
    2. Two (2) members of the State Board of Education
    3. Two (2) members each for the Wayne State University Board of Governors, the Michigan State University Board of Trustees, and the University of Michigan Board of Regents
