Washtenaw County Republican Party


Dear friends,

It's time to get ready to put our best foot forward and get "out among-em".

We've already participated in the Ann Arbor and Whitmore Lake Fourth of July parades (go HERE to take a look at a little photo-journal from the Ann Arbor parade), and now we're getting ready for festivals in Ann Arbor, Chelsea, and Ypsilanti.

To volunteer for these events, just go to our website  www.wash-gop.com , look on the right hand side of the page, where you'll see a little "Volunteer Events" box, click on the event you would like to volunteer for, and sign up.

Our on-line app works just the way it did last year:

  • If you see a Nickname, then all you have to do is click on the particular time slot (or slots) that you are interested in volunteering for, and that's it.

  • If you aren't connected to our app, click on the Connect link, and you'll be asked for your e-mail address

    • If we already know you, because you volunteered last year or you have a login on our site, then you'll see a nickname appear, and you'll be able to click on one of the "Open" time slot interval to sign up for the event.

    • If we don't know you, you'll be asked to fill out a form and we'll get back to you quickly with the information you need in order to volunteer for events.


This is an interesting political year, and it will be both instructive and fun to
learn what our fellow citizens are thinking during this cycle.

So come on out and be a part of the process.

John K. Taylor
