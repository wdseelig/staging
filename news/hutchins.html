You are cordially invited to a very special event!

Why We Are (politically) Conservative
A Panel Conversation with Jewish Leaders

On March 16th, a week from this coming Wednesday, we will be co-hosting an event on the University of Michigan Campus.  The event, a panel with U of M Regent Andrea Fischer Newman, MRP Vice Chair Jeff Sakwa, and Mackinac Center Economist David Littman, will be held at the University of Michigan's Hutchins Hall from 7PM to 9PM.

To clarify our previous event notice, this event is open to everyone and we encourage your attendance.

So by all means come if you would like.  You'll get  a chance to hear, first hand, what students are thinking and saying these days as well as an opportunity to listen to three conservative leaders talk about their political philosophy.

Hope to see you there!
