Washtenaw County Republican Party


Annual Summer Picnic

Hello there!

Our planning is really coming along for our Summer Picnic on August 21st. We've added a number of fun & exciting activities that we hope you'll enjoy.

Time is short though as we need to get totals in for the caterers to make sure we'll have enough food on hand. So Register Today!

As you've seen if you visited the link to sign up for the picnic, (http://www.wash-gop.com/2016picnic), we're also asking people to consider joining the Washtenaw County Republican Party at the same time. We think that there are a lot of good reasons for joining the County Party; here are three of them:
  • First, it is not a huge amount of money, only $30.
  • Second, you get some immediate benefits, including reductions in the price of meals at the picnic.
  • Third, it provides a way for you to amplify your voice and make yourself heard in local political matters in Washtenaw County.
That third point deserves some explanation.  We expect that the cost of holding this event will be just covered by food purchases.  In other words, we don't expect to make any money on the picnic itself.

Membership sales, though, will provide us with funds that we need to support Republican candidates all around the county, and there are many candidates that we would like to help this year.  We in the county party are taking on the job of collecting what we hope will be a lot of small donations and then directing them to worthy candidates.  Helping campaigns by gathering many small donations seems like a better and more democratic way of supporting candidates than relying on a few large donors.

We can't do this, though, without your of support.

So please, as you sign up for our picnic, give serious consideration to also becoming a member of the Washtenaw County Republican Party.
