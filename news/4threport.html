Our 4th of July Parades
I'm happy to report that we had good attendance at both the Ann Arbor and Whitmore Lake parades that we participated in today.

Pictured below are some of the snap shots we were able to capture of our contingent in the Ann Arbor parade. Many thanks go out to those who took the time out of their day to help us celebrate our nation's independence, as well as, support the county party, and the various candidate campaigns.


Although we did get a few boos, as might be expected in Ann Arbor, we enjoyed the many supportive exclamations and gestures we received from the crowd. What's most important though is we had a great time! 


While organizing events such as this can often seem like a daunting task, the completion always reveals the much anticipated reward.

That reward lies in the opportunity to spend time with all the great folks who turn out for our events. We have a wonderful Republican community in Washtenaw County. I'm proud and grateful to be a part of such a special group of people, and I fervently hope that you feel the same way.

To see other photos of our Ann Arbor parade go to our website photo gallery at:  http://www.wash-gop.com/AAFourth
