Washtenaw County Republican Party


Help Defeat Prop 1 May 3rd


As you are most likely aware the Washtenaw County Republican Committee has taken a position of opposition to the the upcoming tax increase on the May 3rd ballot called Prop 1.      

This will be a low turn-out election and the proponents of this tax increase are relying on the fact that you and many other voters won't know about or care to attend this election.

We need to prove them wrong!

Washtenaw County is the most heavily taxed county in the State Of Michigan. Even given that fact, the county is currently being sued for the illegal imposition of property taxes for years in direct violation of our State Constitution.

They're simply out of control!

This current tax is a 38% increase in the operating budget of the Washtenaw Intermediate School District (WISD). An entity many would argue is just a vehicle for getting around our Constitution which limits tax increases that school districts can request.

They call this a tax to fund the education of Special Needs students. The proponents even admit however, there will be absolutely NO increase in spending for Special Education.

It's a ruse to gain your sympathy, don't fall for it!

The WISD Proposal 1 millage request on the ballot here in our county is just the most recent grab for the hard earned money of the citizens of Washtenaw County (see below for more tax increases this year).

Even if some might be able to afford another tax increase it will fall the hardest on those of fixed and low incomes. Those least capable of paying this new tax increase.

Help us Stop this New & Unnecessary Tax Increase!


Help us get out the vote, Here's what you can do:
  1. Join us for phone banking or literature drops to help get-out-the-vote, register here: http://www.wash-gop.com/prop1 
  2. Contact your family, friends, neighbors and aquaintances here in our county via social media, email or phone. Inform them of the upcoming election (many will be unaware) and ask them to vote NO May 3rd. 
  3. Ask those you contact to do the same and contact everyone they know. Forward this email to them.
  4. Make sure you vote May 3rd or get an absentee ballot if you'll be away. Yo can easily get an absentee ballot from your city or township clerk's office. Visit the following link to find an Absentee Ballot Application:    http://www.michigan.gov/documents/AbsentVoterBallot_105377_7.pdf
     
With everyone working together we can defeat this new tax.
