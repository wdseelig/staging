Presidential Primary Election Night Party

Come out and Join us for an Election Night Party,  March 8th, 2016 at the Dexter Pub. Starting at 8:00 P.M., Tuesday evening we'll be there to watch the returns as they come in.  Enjoy a fun evening of interesting discussions about our Presidential Primary candidates. We're starting the evening off late to allow those working the polls time to get there and join the festivities after the polls close.

Folks can order from the menu throughout the night and a cash bar will be available for persons interested.  We encourage all attending to support the venue as they are not charging us for the room use, so dinner and drink sales will be their only source of revenue.
